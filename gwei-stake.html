How it works. Every swap on the $GM pool is taxed in ETH. 50% of that tax goes to the treasury (fuel for the gas-prediction app & planned buybacks), 50% streams to stakers pro-rata in ETH over a 24h window. The tax starts at 20% while the cap is small to bootstrap the protocol and auto-tapers to 5% as marketcap grows.   Penalty. Unstaking re-arms a 100% early-exit penalty that decays linearly to 0 over 7 days; whatever you forfeit is redistributed to the stakers who stay.   Non-custodial. stake / unstake / claim are direct on-chain calls to the hook contract — no intermediary holds your funds.
